How do you feel about OpenAI?

We’re at the “what the heck’s happening!?” point on the AI S-Curve. Our team wants to find out how the learning design community feels about these tools, and begin a discussion on using them more effectively (or perhaps we’ll find out why not to use them!)

This interactive learning experience is built in Twine and uses xAPI. Our objective is to help professionals try out chatGPT in a structured way. We'll collect data about their learning experience.

Information collected and disclaimer

Our team needs to collect a minimum amount of information about you and your interactions with this course in order to share back with the xAPI cohort. We want to be transparent with you about what we’re collecting:

  • Each click (e.g. “Next” buttons)
  • Survey responses
  • Text input

To achieve this we'll set a cookie with an anonymous identifier in your browser to track you as an Actor — for example, user51650404. This way we can gather data on individual learners without collecting any personally identifiable information. When you click Launch activity we will set that cookie.
